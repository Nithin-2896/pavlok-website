Pavlok Bands: Custom Fit for Your Habit-Breaking Journey

A great wearable experience relies on a quality band. Whether you use a Pavlok 2 Band or need a replacement strap, comfort and stability play a crucial role in your habit-breaking success.

Choose the Right Band for You

Pavlok devices deliver timely feedback—vibrations, beeps, or zaps—to help you overcome bad habits. The classic Pavlok 2 Band is designed for a secure, comfortable fit, available in various colors to suit your style.

If you desire a change, the Pavlok Shock Band optimizes feedback with enhanced comfort, while the Pavlok Watch Band offers a modern, sleek look along with robust functionality.

Replacement and Upgrade Options

Whether your band has worn out or you're ready to upgrade, Pavlok offers a range of replacement straps—including the Pavlok 2 Replacement Band and the improved Shock Band 2.0. For a premium feel, check out the Pavlok Milanese Band with its refined mesh-metal design.

Lost Your Band?

If you've misplaced your original Pavlok band, replacement options are available via Pavlok’s support page. You can choose either an identical match or explore upgrades.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pavlok Bands

Q: What if I lose my Pavlok band?
Pavlok offers a variety of replacement straps that can be ordered online and delivered promptly.
Q: What’s the difference between a Pavlok band and a rubber band?
Pavlok bands are engineered to deliver haptic feedback (beeps and zaps) and sync with the app, unlike ordinary rubber bands.

Why Your Pavlok Band Matters

A secure, stylish, and high-performing band is essential to get the most out of your Pavlok device. Choose the band that best suits your style and habit‑breaking needs.

Looking Ahead: Your Habit-Breaking Future

Embrace your journey with the right Pavlok band. Explore new options, upgrade when necessary, and let your wearable empower your lasting transformation.
